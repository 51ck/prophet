# Vision

## Problem (locked)

The seeker has a problem or question they cannot resolve or answer by ordinary means — logic, advice, waiting, or everyday decision tools have not been enough. They **want** an esoteric answer: cards, ritual, omen — not another rational pep talk.

We help them get that reading without wasting their time or money: short path to a proper question, honest ritual, clear close. No endless chat tax, no fake cards dressed as destiny, no expensive theater that replaces substance.

## What

Tarot Prophet is an authentic AI tarot reader present on Telegram. It behaves as a prophet: helps a seeker lock the question they already need answered esoterically, then performs a real ritual with a real deck state — shuffle, layout, draw, open — and interprets what the cards actually show.

## Why

- **Job to be done:** esoteric resolution when normal routes fail and the seeker chooses that path
- **Efficiency:** save time and money vs drifting chat, repeated empty readings, or costly inauthentic alternatives
- **Authenticity claim:** most AI tarot invents cards to fit a story; this product inverts that — ritual produces the cards; the prophet interprets

## Who

- **Seeker** — person with an unresolved question who wants an esoteric answer (DM or via group summon into a reading path)
- **Prophet** — agentic guide: clarifies intent, chooses or confirms deck when needed, runs ritual, asks and answers during the reading, closes cleanly

## Authenticity bar

- Deck state is real: order, orientation, and face-up/down matter and are in principle inspectable
- Outcomes come only from ritual operations — the prophet does not invent which cards appear
- Interpretation is agentic; card identity and order are not
- Conversation serves the reading; it does not replace the ritual

## Multi-deck

Several decks may exist as named, fully described systems. Preference/settings can fix a deck; when unset or when the reading calls for it, the prophet may choose. See [deck-and-ritual.md](deck-and-ritual.md).

## Session spirit

Conversation-first but bounded: help the seeker find a proper question, then ritual. During ritual, seeker and prophet may still question each other. See [session.md](session.md).

## Surfaces

Primary intimacy in direct messages. Summonable in group chats as presence and a path into a reading — etiquette and privacy rules in [telegram-ux.md](telegram-ux.md).

## Economics stance

Sustainable by design; paid access (likely Telegram Stars) comes after the ritual and UX feel are proven. See [economics.md](economics.md) and [roadmap.md](roadmap.md).

## Non-goals (idea stage)

- Replacing therapy, legal, medical, or other ordinary professional advice the seeker still needs
- Pushing esoteric answers on people who want ordinary problem-solving
- Endless freeform companionship chat as the main product
- Fabricated spreads that skip honest deck state
- Settling monetization or exact button UX before the reading feel exists
