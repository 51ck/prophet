# Session

## Arc

Conversation-first, then ritual. Length of talk is limited so the session stays a reading, not an open-ended chat.

1. **Intake (short)** — Prophet helps the seeker find a *proper question*: clear, honest, workable for cards. Few turns; no wandering forever.
2. **Commit** — Question locks. Ritual may begin. Deck is confirmed (preference, explicit choice, or prophet choice).
3. **Ritual** — Shuffle with genuine physical-analogue operations → choose/apply spread → draw into positions → open cards (face-down until opened). Deck state stays honest throughout.
4. **Mid-ritual dialogue** — Seeker and prophet may ask each other questions (clarify a position, a feeling, a choice to open next) without inventing or rewriting cards already determined by ritual.
5. **Closing** — Synthesis of the opened layout against the locked question. Session ends cleanly. Further talk is a new session (or an explicit short coda), not endless drift.

## Proper question

The seeker’s starting point: something they cannot settle ordinarily, and that they want answered esoterically. Intake shapes that into a *proper question*:

- Specific enough that cards can speak to it
- Honest about what the seeker actually wants to know
- Workable (not a demand that cards “prove” a fixed answer)
- Oriented to omen/counsel — not a substitute for ordinary steps they still refuse to take when those are clearly enough

Prophet steers toward that during intake, keeping the path short (time saved). If the seeker cannot lock a question, prophet may defer or end rather than fake a reading.

## Bounds

- Intake has a soft cap on turns / length; after that, prophet pushes to lock, defer, or stop
- Once the question is locked, changing it mid-ritual is exceptional and must be explicit (and may restart or reframe the spread — product rule: prefer finish-then-new-session)
- Closing ends the reading; companionship after that is out of scope for the core arc

## Mid-ritual Q&A

Allowed:

- Seeker asks what a position means before or after a card opens
- Prophet asks how a card lands with the seeker’s situation
- Seeker asks to open a particular face-down card next (if the ritual pacing allows)
- Clarifying the locked question’s nuance without replacing it

Not allowed:

- Prophet inventing a different card than the deck state holds
- Quietly reshuffling away an unwanted outcome to please the seeker
- Turning ritual into unbounded therapy chat with no relation to the layout

## States (conceptual)

| State | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Idle | No active reading |
| Intake | Finding / shaping the question |
| Committed | Question locked; ritual about to run or running |
| Ritual | Shuffle / layout / draw / open in progress |
| Closing | Synthesis and farewell |
| Ended | Session finished |
