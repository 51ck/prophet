# Spreads

## Principles

- Match spread size to question complexity
- Prefer fewer positions when the question is sharp
- Positions have roles; cards fill them by ritual draw (usually face-down until open)

## Phase 1 — three-card (default shape)

**Name:** Three Roads (working name)

| Position | Role |
|----------|------|
| 1 | Situation — how the matter sits now |
| 2 | Counsel — what the cards advise holding or doing |
| 3 | Path — likely unfolding if counsel is taken / energy to watch |

Use for most locked questions in Phase 1.

## Phase 1 — sharp question (optional)

**Single Focus** — one card when the question is very sharp (one hinge).

**Two Poles** — two cards when the seeker holds a clear either/or (pole A / pole B), without forcing a fake binary if the question is not binary.

Prophet picks among these after Commit; seeker may ask for fewer or more within reason.

## Later

Richer named spreads (e.g. longer situational layouts) land in later phases. Do not invent a huge catalog before ritual feel is proven.
